Best Resources for Sales Enablement Professionals in 2026

Sales enablement owns the intersection of content, training, and technology that makes sellers effective. The role has expanded from content management to encompass onboarding, coaching, analytics, and AI-assisted selling.

This list covers the communities, platforms, and content that enablement practitioners actually use. No vendor rankings disguised as editorial.

Newsletters

1. Sales Enablement Collective Newsletter

Newsletter with insights and resources, reaching 18,000+ enablement professionals.

Blogs & Websites

1. Spekit Blog

Revenue enablement trends, AI strategies, and just-in-time enablement content.

2. Highspot Resource Library

Guides, templates, and Gartner reports from a leading enablement platform.

3. Seismic Resources

Product releases, research reports, and enablement content from the leading enablement platform.

4. FFWD Sales Enablement

Felix Krueger's consulting firm with 'This Month In Sales Enablement' content roundups.

5. SE Nablers OUR PICK

Sales enablement professional community and career resources.

Communities

1. Sales Enablement Collective

18,000+ member community with newsletter, podcast, summits, and resources library.

2. Revenue Enablement Society

Not-for-profit professional membership association with chapter programs and free community.

Podcasts

1. Stories From The Trenches Podcast

RES podcast where enablement practitioners share real-world experiences, hosted by Paul Butterfield.

2. The State of Sales Enablement Podcast

Monthly podcast by Felix Krueger with actionable advice from B2B enablement leaders.

How We Curated This List

Three criteria. First, does this resource teach you something you can't learn from a Google search? Second, is it actively maintained and producing new content? Third, do practitioners in the role actually recommend it to peers? We don't accept payment for listings. We review and update this page quarterly.
